Death

Long summer afternoon,
In dream I had 12 cookies,
I ate 10 and put 2 in my pocket.
Now that I have woken up,
I can't find them.

Life

Long winter night,
Waiting for the last bus I said to myself,
"Have I missed the bus?".
Well, there is tomorrow.
The bus will return.

Love

I smiled.
